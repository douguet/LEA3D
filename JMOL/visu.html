Click on a ligand name (in the right table) to visualize the complex

Interface control commands:

Rotation: mouse left button
Zoom up: shift + mouse left button
Visualization option: mouse right button

To remove hydrogens: mouse right button click, select style menu, then select atoms menu: deselect hydrogens display

Measures (distances, angles and torsions): double click with the mouse left button then click on the other atoms (second: distance, third: angle, fourth: torsion)
